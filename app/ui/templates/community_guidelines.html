Community Guidelines

Last updated: {{ policy_last_updated }}

Be respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or targeted abuse.
  • No hateful content or attacks based on protected characteristics.
  • Do not encourage self-harm or violence.

Keep it safe

  • No sexual exploitation or content involving minors.
  • No sharing private or identifying information about someone without permission (doxxing).
  • No explicit sexual content where it is not allowed by the product settings and local laws.

Be honest

  • No impersonation intended to mislead or defraud.
  • No scams, phishing, or attempts to steal accounts or money.
  • No spam or manipulation (mass posting, fake engagement, or coordinated abuse).

Content and moderation

We may remove content or restrict accounts that violate these rules. Repeated or severe violations may result in suspension or permanent removal.

Reporting

If you see something that violates these guidelines, report it using the in-app tools where available.
